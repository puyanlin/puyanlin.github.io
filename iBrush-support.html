常見問題 FAQ FAQ

  • 需要登入嗎？ 不需要。這個 App 不建立帳號，透過 iCloud 在同一 Apple ID 下同步。
  • Do I need to sign in? No. This app does not create accounts and syncs via iCloud under the same Apple ID.
  • 我的資料存在哪裡？ 您的刷牙記錄資料儲存在 iCloud 的私人資料庫（CloudKit Private DB），只有您可存取。i 刷的共享功能會將共享資料存在 iCloud 共享資料庫中，僅限共享發起者與受邀者能檢視。
  • Where is my data stored? Your brushing record data is stored in your iCloud Private Database (CloudKit Private DB) and only you can access it. iBrush's sharing feature stores shared data in iCloud Shared Database, accessible only to the share creator and invitees.
  • 刷牙偵測如何運作？ i 刷使用 iOS Core ML 機器學習模型在您的設備本地偵測刷牙動作，不會將資料上傳到網路。您可以選擇是否協助改善偵測模型。
  • How does brushing detection work? iBrush uses iOS Core ML machine learning models to detect brushing locally on your device, without uploading data to the internet. You can choose whether to help improve the detection model.
  • 是否會顯示廣告？ 是，為維護服務，我們使用廣告支援 App 的免費提供。
  • Will the app show ads? Yes. To keep the service sustainable, we use ads to support offering the app for free.
