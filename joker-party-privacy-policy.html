Joker Party – 隱私權政策 Privacy Policy

最後更新 Last Updated: 2025-Jan-16

中文版

「Joker Party」重視您的隱私。本政策說明我們如何處理資料。

我們收集或使用的資料

  • Game Center / GameKit：用於多人遊戲配對、同播共享（SharePlay）功能以及玩家身份識別。Apple GameKit 會處理玩家的 Game Center 暱稱、頭像與遊戲數據，這些資料由 Apple 管理，開發者無法存取您的個人身份資訊。
  • 廣告（Google Mobile Ads / AdMob）：為提供免費服務，我們展示廣告。廣告供應商可能蒐集裝置資訊（如 IDFA）以投遞、衡量與防止不正常流量。您可在 iOS【設定 ▸ 隱私權與安全性 ▸ 追蹤】管理追蹤許可，或重設廣告識別碼。

我們不會

  • 建立帳號或要求登入；遊戲使用您的 Game Center 身份進行配對與連線。
  • 將您的個人內容上傳到開發者自有伺服器；遊戲數據僅透過 Apple GameKit 在玩家之間傳遞。
  • 發送推播通知；本 App 不使用推播功能。

資料保留與安全

  • 您可隨時刪除 App；遊戲不會在裝置外保存個人遊戲記錄。
  • GameKit 之傳輸與儲存安全由 Apple 提供；我們不會另行上傳到第三方伺服器。

第三方服務

  • Apple Game Center / GameKit（多人遊戲配對與同播共享）
  • Google Mobile Ads SDK（廣告投放與衡量）

年齡分級

本 App 分類為 4+，適合所有年齡層使用。

聯絡我們

若對本政策有疑問，請來信：puyan.tw@icloud.com

English

Joker Party ("the App") values your privacy. This policy explains how your data is handled.

Data we collect/use

  • Game Center / GameKit: Used for multiplayer matchmaking, SharePlay functionality, and player identity. Apple GameKit handles players' Game Center nicknames, avatars, and game data. This data is managed by Apple; the developer cannot access your personal identifying information.
  • Ads (Google Mobile Ads / AdMob): To keep the App free, we serve ads. The provider may collect device info (IDFA, coarse location, model, IP) for delivery, measurement, and fraud prevention. You can manage App Tracking Transparency in iOS settings or reset the advertising identifier.

We do not

  • Create accounts or require sign-in; the game uses your Game Center identity for matchmaking and connection.
  • Upload your personal content to developer-run servers; game data is only transmitted between players via Apple GameKit.
  • Send push notifications; this App does not use push functionality.

Retention & Security

  • You may delete the App anytime; the game does not store personal game records outside your device.
  • Transport and security are provided by Apple GameKit; we do not upload to other third-party servers.

Third-Party Services

  • Apple Game Center / GameKit (multiplayer matchmaking & SharePlay)
  • Google Mobile Ads SDK (ads & measurement)

Age Rating

The App is rated 4+ and is suitable for all ages.
