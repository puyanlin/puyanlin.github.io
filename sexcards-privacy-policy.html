中文版

「慾火時光」（SexCards）重視您的隱私。本政策說明我們如何處理資料。

我們收集或使用的資料

  • 本機儲存（UserDefaults）：自訂卡牌組、卡片內容與遊戲進度等資料儲存在您的裝置上，僅存於裝置本地，開發者無法存取。
  • 相簿存取：當您為自訂卡片添加圖片時，App 會請求存取您的相簿。所選圖片僅儲存於裝置本地，不會上傳至任何伺服器。
  • 廣告（Google Mobile Ads / AdMob）：為提供免費服務，我們展示橫幅與插頁式廣告。廣告供應商可能蒐集裝置資訊（如 IDFA）以投遞、衡量與防止不正常流量。我們會依 Apple 規定請求「App 追蹤透明度」（ATT）；您可在 iOS【設定 ▸ 隱私權與安全性 ▸ 追蹤】管理追蹤許可，或重設廣告識別碼。

我們不會

  • 建立帳號或要求登入；本 App 不需另行註冊或登入。
  • 將您的卡片或遊戲資料上傳到開發者自有伺服器；資料僅存於您的裝置。
  • 發送推播通知；本 App 不使用推播功能。
  • 蒐集或儲存任何與您的親密活動相關的個人資訊。

應用程式內購買

本 App 提供應用程式內購買（去除廣告）。購買由 Apple 處理，我們不會另行蒐集與購買直接相關的個人身份資訊。

資料保留與安全

  • 您可隨時刪除 App；本地資料會隨之移除。
  • 我們不會將您的任何資料上傳到第三方伺服器。

第三方服務

  • Google Mobile Ads SDK（廣告投放與衡量；受 ATT 規範）

年齡分級與內容聲明

本 App 分級為 18+（限制級），含有性暗示內容，僅供成年人使用。未滿 18 歲者請勿下載或使用本 App。

English

SexCards ("the App") values your privacy. This policy explains how your data is handled.

Data we collect/use

  • On-Device Storage (UserDefaults): Custom card decks, card content, and game progress are stored on your device. Data stays on your device only; the developer cannot access it.
  • Photo Library Access: When you add images to custom cards, the App requests access to your photo library. Selected images are stored locally on your device only and are never uploaded to any server.
  • Ads (Google Mobile Ads / AdMob): To keep the App free, we serve banner and interstitial ads. The provider may collect device info (e.g. IDFA) for delivery, measurement, and fraud prevention. We request App Tracking Transparency (ATT) as required by Apple; you can manage tracking in iOS Settings → Privacy & Security → Tracking, or reset the advertising identifier.

We do not

  • Create accounts or require sign-in; this App does not require separate registration or login.
  • Upload your cards or game data to developer-run servers; data remains on your device only.
  • Send push notifications; this App does not use push.
  • Collect or store any personal information related to your intimate activities.

In-App Purchases

The App offers an in-app purchase to remove ads. Purchases are processed by Apple; we do not separately collect personal identifying information tied to those purchases.

Retention & Security

  • You may delete the App anytime; local data will be removed with it.
  • We do not upload any of your data to third-party servers.

Third-Party Services

  • Google Mobile Ads SDK (ads & measurement; subject to ATT)

Age Rating & Content Disclaimer

This App is rated 18+ (Restricted) and contains sexually suggestive content. It is intended for adults only. Persons under 18 should not download or use this App.
