中文版

「奇貼」（KidStickers）與「一起貼」（TogetherStickers）重視您的隱私。本政策說明我們如何處理資料。

我們收集或使用的資料

  • iCloud 私有資料庫（CloudKit Private DB）：您的貼紙簿資料（孩子暱稱、貼紙與獎勵設定等）會以結構化格式儲存在您 Apple ID 名下的 iCloud 私有資料庫。只有您本人可存取，開發者無法檢視。
  • iCloud 共享（CloudKit Shared DB）：「一起貼」支援 iCloud 共享功能，讓您與他人共享貼紙簿。共享資料同樣以結構化格式儲存，僅限共享發起者與受邀者能檢視，開發者無法存取。
  • 推播通知權限：用於資料同步事件通知。

我們不會

  • 建立帳號或要求登入。
  • 將您的個人內容上傳到開發者自有伺服器；資料只會存在於您的 iCloud 私有資料庫或您參與的共享資料庫中。
  • 讀取您未主動選取的照片；僅在您挑選頭像或貼紙圖片時存取，並只做圓形裁切與壓縮後儲存。

資料保留與安全

  • 您可隨時刪除 App；資料仍存於您的 iCloud，直到您於 iCloud 介面或於重新安裝後在 App 內刪除。
  • CloudKit 之傳輸與儲存安全由 Apple 提供；我們不會另行上傳到第三方伺服器。

第三方服務

  • Apple iCloud / CloudKit（私有儲存與同步）

兒童隱私

本 App 供親子共同使用；我們不要求兒童輸入個人身分資訊。請家長妥善管理裝置與 iCloud 帳號。

English

KidStickers and TogetherStickers ("the Apps") value your privacy. This policy explains how your data is handled.

Data we collect/use

  • iCloud CloudKit Private DB: Your sticker book data (child nicknames, stickers, rewards) is stored in a structured format in your iCloud Private Database. Only you can access it; the developer cannot view it.
  • iCloud CloudKit Shared DB: TogetherStickers supports iCloud sharing, allowing you to share sticker books with others. Shared data is also stored in a structured format. Only the share creator and invitees can view it; the developer cannot access it.
  • Push Notifications: Used for data sync notifications.

We do not

  • Create accounts or require sign-in.
  • Upload your personal content to developer-run servers; your data stays in your iCloud Private Database or shared databases you participate in.
  • Access photos you did not explicitly pick; selected images are cropped into a circle and compressed for storage.

Retention & Security

  • You may delete the App anytime; data remains in iCloud until you remove it via iCloud or within the App after reinstall.
  • Transport and at-rest security are provided by Apple CloudKit; we do not upload to other third-party servers.

Third-Party Services

  • Apple iCloud / CloudKit (private storage & sync)

Children's Privacy

The Apps are intended for family use. We do not ask children to submit personal identifying information. Parents should manage the device and iCloud account appropriately.
