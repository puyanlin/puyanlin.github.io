常見問題 FAQ FAQ

  • 需要登入嗎？ 不需要。本 App 不另建帳號或要求登入，所有資料僅存於您的裝置。
  • Do I need to sign in? No. This app does not create accounts or require login; all data stays on your device.
  • 資料存在哪裡？ 徽章與獎懲記錄儲存在您 iPhone 或 Apple Watch 的本機（SwiftData），僅在您自己的裝置間同步，開發者無法存取。
  • Where is my data stored? Badge and reward/punishment records are stored locally on your iPhone or Apple Watch (SwiftData), and sync only between your own devices; the developer cannot access them.
  • 是否會顯示廣告？ 是。為維持免費提供，我們使用廣告；您可透過應用程式內購買移除廣告。
  • Will the app show ads? Yes. To keep the app free, we show ads; you can remove them via an in-app purchase.
  • Apple Watch 可以做什麼？ 您可在 Watch 上查看徽章與記錄。若購買「解鎖 Watch 編輯功能」，即可在 Watch 上新增或編輯好表現／壞表現與徽章。
  • What can I do on Apple Watch? You can view badges and records on the Watch. If you purchase "Unlock Watch editing", you can add or edit good/bad behavior and badges on the Watch.
  • 應用程式內購買有哪些？ 包含「去除廣告」以及「解鎖 Apple Watch 編輯功能」等，可在 App 內商店查看與購買。
  • What in-app purchases are available? These include "Remove ads" and "Unlock Apple Watch editing", among others; you can view and purchase them in the in-app store.
