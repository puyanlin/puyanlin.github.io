常見問題 FAQ FAQ

  • 如何使用貼圖？ 在 iMessage 對話中，點擊輸入框旁的 App 圖示，選擇貼圖包，然後點選或拖曳貼圖即可傳送。
  • How to use stickers? In an iMessage conversation, tap the App icon next to the text field, select the sticker pack, then tap or drag a sticker to send it.
  • 需要登入嗎？ 不需要。本 App 不建立帳號，也不需要任何登入或註冊。
  • Do I need to sign in? No. This app does not create accounts and requires no sign-in or registration.
  • 會收集我的資料嗎？ 不會。這是純本地運作的貼圖包，不連接網路，不收集、儲存或傳輸任何個人資料。
  • Does it collect my data? No. This is a purely local sticker pack that does not connect to the internet and does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data.
  • 可以退費嗎？ 所有購買與退款由 Apple 處理。如需申請退款，請前往 reportaproblem.apple.com。
  • Can I get a refund? All purchases and refunds are handled by Apple. To request a refund, please visit reportaproblem.apple.com.
