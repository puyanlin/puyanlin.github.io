常見問題 FAQ FAQ

  • 需要登入嗎？ 不需要。本 App 不建立帳號，所有資料都存在您的裝置上。
  • Do I need to sign in? No. The app does not create accounts; all data is kept on your device.
  • 陀螺軌跡是怎麼產生的？ App 使用 Apple Vision、AVFoundation 與 Core Image 在您的裝置本地分析影片、產生粒子軌跡，不會將影片上傳到網路。
  • How are the trails generated? The app uses Apple Vision, AVFoundation, and Core Image to analyse the clip and generate particle trails entirely on your device. Videos are never uploaded.
  • 處理完的影片儲存在哪裡？ 訂閱用戶的影片會存到 App 內的「對戰回顧」與「照片」相簿（預設名稱「陀螺對戰相機」）。未訂閱用戶生成的影片在離開結果頁時會被丟棄。
  • Where are processed clips stored? For Pro subscribers, generated clips land in the in-app "Battle Review" archive and your Photos album ("陀螺對戰相機" by default). For non-subscribers, the clip is discarded once you leave the result screen.
  • Top Match Pro 訂閱是什麼？ 解鎖儲存對戰、Photos 相簿同步、無限生成、搶先體驗新特效包。提供月訂與年訂兩種方案，新訂閱者享 7 天免費試用。
  • What is Top Match Pro? Pro unlocks saving battles, Photos album sync, unlimited renders, and early access to new effect packs. Monthly and yearly plans are available, with a 7-day free trial for new subscribers.
  • 如何取消訂閱？ 請至 iOS【設定 ▸ Apple ID ▸ 訂閱項目】管理或取消。免費試用結束前 24 小時取消即不會收費。
  • How do I cancel my subscription? Open iOS Settings ▸ Apple ID ▸ Subscriptions. Cancelling at least 24 hours before the trial ends means you won't be charged.
  • 還原購買在哪裡？ 設定頁的訂閱區塊或訂閱頁右上角點「還原購買」即可。請確認使用同一組 Apple ID。
  • How do I restore a purchase? Tap "Restore" in the subscription section of Settings or in the top-right of the subscription sheet. Make sure you're signed in with the same Apple ID.
  • App 需要哪些權限？ 相機（錄製對戰）、麥克風（錄音）、照片（讀取選取的影片並寫回相簿）。可在 iOS【設定 ▸ 隱私權與安全性】管理。
  • What permissions does the app need? Camera (record battles), microphone (audio capture), and Photos (read selected videos / save processed clips). Manage them under iOS Settings ▸ Privacy & Security.
  • 影片處理很慢？ 影片越長處理越久。可至設定頁將輸出解析度調為 720p 以加快速度，請在處理中保持 App 開啟。
  • Processing takes too long? Longer clips take longer. Lower the output resolution to 720p in Settings to speed things up, and keep the app open while it renders.
