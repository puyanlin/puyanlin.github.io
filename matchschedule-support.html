常見問題 FAQ FAQ

  • 需要登入嗎？ 不需要。App 不需要任何帳號或登入，直接開始建立賽程。
  • Do I need to sign in? No. The app requires no account or sign-in — just open and start creating tournaments.
  • 免費版有什麼限制？ 免費版最多可建立 2 個賽程。觀看獎勵廣告可額外解鎖賽程名額，或購買「永久移除廣告」方案解鎖無限賽程。
  • What are the free tier limits? Free users can create up to 2 tournaments. Watch a rewarded ad to unlock extra slots, or purchase the "Remove Ads" upgrade for unlimited tournaments.
  • 如何移除廣告？ 在「設定」中購買「永久移除廣告」方案（App 內購買），即可永久關閉所有廣告並解鎖無限賽程。
  • How do I remove ads? Purchase "Remove Ads" in Settings (in-app purchase) to permanently disable all ads and unlock unlimited tournaments.
  • 如何還原購買？ 前往「設定 ▸ Premium」，點擊「還原購買」，使用相同 Apple ID 登入後即可恢復已購買的方案。
  • How do I restore my purchase? Go to Settings ▸ Premium and tap "Restore Purchases." Sign in with the same Apple ID used for the original purchase.
  • 資料會備份到雲端嗎？ 不會。所有賽程資料僅儲存在您的裝置上，刪除 App 後資料將一併移除。
  • Is my data backed up to the cloud? No. All tournament data is stored only on your device. Deleting the app removes all data.
  • 可以匯出賽程圖片嗎？ 可以。在賽程頁面點擊分享按鈕，即可將對戰表匯出為圖片並儲存至相簿或分享。首次使用需授權相簿存取權限。
  • Can I export the bracket as an image? Yes. Tap the share button on the bracket view to export the bracket as an image. You'll be prompted for photo library access the first time.
  • 支援 Apple TV（tvOS）嗎？ 支援。tvOS 版本提供完整的賽程瀏覽體驗，不顯示廣告。
  • Is Apple TV (tvOS) supported? Yes. The tvOS version offers a full bracket viewing experience with no ads.
  • App 會追蹤我嗎？ iOS 首次啟動時會詢問追蹤授權（ATT）。若您拒絕，廣告將改為非個人化廣告，App 功能完全不受影響。tvOS 不進行追蹤。
  • Does the app track me? On iOS, the app asks for App Tracking Transparency (ATT) authorization on first launch. If you decline, ads switch to non-personalized; app functionality is unaffected. tvOS does not track.
