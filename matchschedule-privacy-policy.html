最後更新 Last Updated: 2026-Apr-15

資料儲存 Data Storage

所有賽程資料（隊伍名稱、比分、主題設定、封面圖片等）僅儲存於您的裝置本機，不會上傳至開發者或任何第三方伺服器。

All tournament data (team names, scores, theme settings, cover images, etc.) is stored locally on your device only. Nothing is uploaded to developer or third-party servers.

我們收集或使用的資料 Data we collect / use

  • 廣告（Google Mobile Ads / AdMob）— 僅限 iOS：為提供免費服務，iOS 版本展示橫幅、插頁式及獎勵廣告。廣告供應商可能蒐集裝置資訊（如廣告識別碼 IDFA、裝置型號、IP 位址）以投遞廣告、衡量成效及防止廣告詐欺。您可在 iOS【設定 ▸ 隱私權與安全性 ▸ 追蹤】管理追蹤許可，或重設廣告識別碼。tvOS 版本不顯示廣告。
  • Ads (Google Mobile Ads / AdMob) — iOS only: To keep the app free, the iOS version shows banner, interstitial, and rewarded ads. The ad provider may collect device info (advertising identifier IDFA, device model, IP address) for ad delivery, measurement, and fraud prevention. You can manage App Tracking Transparency in iOS Settings ▸ Privacy & Security ▸ Tracking, or reset your advertising identifier. The tvOS version shows no ads.
  • App 追蹤透明度（ATT）— 僅限 iOS：App 首次啟動時會請求追蹤授權。若您拒絕，廣告商將自動改為非個人化廣告；App 功能不受影響。
  • App Tracking Transparency (ATT) — iOS only: On first launch, the app requests tracking authorization. If you decline, the ad provider automatically switches to non-personalized ads; app functionality is unaffected.
  • App 內購買（IAP）：透過 Apple App Store 購買「永久移除廣告」方案時，付款與交易記錄由 Apple 全權處理，開發者無法存取您的付款資訊。
  • In-App Purchases (IAP): When purchasing the "Remove Ads" upgrade through the Apple App Store, payment and transaction records are handled entirely by Apple. The developer cannot access your payment information.
  • 照片圖庫：匯出賽程圖片時，App 會請求相簿寫入權限，僅用於將圖片儲存至您的裝置。
  • Photo Library: When exporting bracket images, the app requests photo library write access solely to save the image to your device.

我們不會 We do not

  • 建立帳號或要求登入。
  • Create accounts or require sign-in.
  • 將賽程資料、照片或個人資訊上傳至任何伺服器。
  • Upload tournament data, photos, or personal information to any server.
  • 發送推播通知。
  • Send push notifications.
  • 在 tvOS 上展示廣告或請求追蹤授權。
  • Show ads or request tracking authorization on tvOS.

資料保留與安全 Retention & Security

  • 所有本機資料（賽程、圖片、主題）隨 App 刪除一併移除。
  • All local data (tournaments, images, themes) is removed when you delete the app.
  • 廣告 SDK 的資料傳輸與安全由 Google 提供；IAP 交易安全由 Apple 提供。
  • Ad SDK data transmission and security are provided by Google; IAP transaction security is provided by Apple.

第三方服務 Third-Party Services

  • Google Mobile Ads SDK（廣告投放與衡量，僅 iOS）
  • Google Mobile Ads SDK (ad delivery & measurement, iOS only)
  • Apple StoreKit / App Store（App 內購買）
  • Apple StoreKit / App Store (in-app purchases)
